EconTwitter at Mastodon

2022-11-13 09:42

Comments

Source

Tags:

  • Mastodon
  • social media

This past week I witnessed a mass migration of economists in the wilds of social media. Over 4,700 individuals moved to EconTwitter, a Mastodon instance set up by Paul Goldsmith-Pinkham. This online community is just getting started, but I'm excited by its potential. There are already over 21,000 posts!

Mastodon is a free and open-sourced microblogging service that is similar to Twitter. The main difference is hosted by self-hosted independent instances (servers) that can interoperate as a federated social network.

You can join me on EconTwitter by following @georgetsilva@econtwitter.net.
